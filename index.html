OUR BELIEF

Networking is why people come to events. But it doesn’t always happen.

People scan the room and stick with who they already know. Most attendees meet one or two people. They miss the conversations that matter. The people worth meeting most aren’t always the ones you naturally end up talking to. That’s not a coincidence — it’s a design problem.

Better events don’t leave connection to chance. They design for it.

That’s why we built Hoogah.

Built by Khushi & Joe. The room they wished existed.
